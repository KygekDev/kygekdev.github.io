About KygekDev

KygekDev is a Minecraft player, PocketMine-MP plugins developer and manager of Minecraft servers. He has many experiences with web programming, Minecraft servers, PocketMine-MP plugins, the Minecraft game and VPS management. He was previously named Kygekraqmak, but he changed his name for readability reasons. He has dedicated thousands of hours to Minecraft servers, PocketMine-MP plugins and KygekTeam. He has helped many people in the KygekTeam Discord server and in private messages. He really focuses on learning new things and be experienced in Minecraft servers and PocketMine-MP plugins, even if sometimes he struggles or fails. He also committed to always learn to improve his quality of work and service.

Self-Taught the PHP Language and Other Things

KygekDev taught himself to learn the PHP language, including OOP, and making basic PocketMine-MP plugins. It was not easy, but with a commitment, he managed to learn the PHP language. He also learnt how to use the Command Prompt in Windows, the Linux Terminal and essential tools and utilities for PocketMine-MP plugin development. He is currently learning to make more complex PocketMine-MP plugins.

KygekDev is also able to make basic websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and PHP. With the help of his friend, aminozomty, he managed to make the KygekTeam website from scratch. The KygekTeam website is still gets constantly updated for better user experience. Currently, he is learning to make more complex websites and planned to remake a new website for KygekTeam.

Founded KygekTeam

KygekTeam initially started as Kygek Development. On July 2020, KygekDev made a Discord server for support and discussions about his plugins, after he published his first PocketMine-MP, KygekJoinUI. The Discord server was named Kygek Development. Then he made more plugins and a website as a downloads and links center to all of his plugins. After he had made enough plugins, had enough users and a staff, he decided to change Kygek Development to KygekTeam. He continued making more plugins and improving the website. He also made a working PHAR Converter, mainly for PocketMine-MP plugins. KygekTeam finally got its own domain on December 2020.

KygekTeam has published many plugins on Poggit and received tens of thousands downloads. KygekTeam also has helped many people with PocketMine-MP plugin development.

Helped Many Minecraft Servers and Others

KygekDev has a long experience with Minecraft servers, mainly PocketMine-MP. He also has made several Minecraft servers and was a staff in several Minecraft servers. He has helped with technical and PocketMine-MP plugins common issues in several Minecraft servers. Currently, he is planning to make a new Minecraft server under KygekTeam.

KygekDev understands how difficult it is to learn making PocketMine-MP plugins and managing technical stuffs related with Minecraft server. That's why he has helped many people, mainly with technical and PocketMine-MP plugins privately and through the KygekTeam Discord server. Currently, he is planning to help many people to learn making a Minecraft server using PocketMine-MP.
